# AIC Bondeni Official Website
This repository contains the source code for the AIC Bondeni church website (aicbondeni.co.ke). 
The platform serves as the digital front door for our congregation, extending our mission, evangelism, worship, and discipleship goals into the digital space.

## Purpose and MotiveThe primary objective of this project is to increase the church's visibility and outreach beyond the physical congregation. 
It is designed to be a mobile-friendly and accessible hub that integrates online visitors into church activities and follow-up systems.

## Core FeaturesThe website is built to support the spiritual and operational needs of the church through the following modules:
About Us: Comprehensive information regarding the church's history and mission.
Sermon Portal: A dedicated space for sermon uploads to ensure the Word is accessible anytime.
Event Management: An integrated calendar to keep the congregation informed of upcoming activities.
Digital Stewardship: A secure online giving portal for tithes and offerings.
Interaction Hubs: Includes contact forms and a dedicated prayer request portal.

## Security and MaintenanceWe prioritize the safety of our community's data through:
- Data Protection: Implementation of cybersecurity and data protection policies.
- Access Control: A digital asset register for secure domain ownership and password management.
- Sustainability: Regular maintenance schedules and backup systems for all digital content.
